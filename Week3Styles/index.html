My Favorite Video Game: Fortnite

Fortnite is my go to game because it is fast, competitive, and always changing.

What it is

Fortnite is an online game where you drop into a match, loot items, and try to be the last player or team standing. Every match feels different because the map and items change over time.

Why I like it

I like it because you can play solo or with friends, and it rewards practice. It also stays interesting because new updates keep changing the meta.

  • Quick matches
  • Fun with friends
  • Always something new

Basic match flow

This is the simple loop I follow almost every game.

  1. Land and loot
  2. Rotate with the storm
  3. Take smart fights late game
